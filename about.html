About

Elisa Rode is the owner and CEO of Kearley & Company — a full‑service marketing and branding firm partnering with financial institutions (especially credit unions) and healthcare clients across the U.S. A Fort Worth native, she joined Kearley in 2003 as a copywriter and strategist, became Vice President in 2008, and purchased the firm in 2010. Today she leads the agency as CEO while continuing to write, consult, speak, and support the digital marketing team.

Elisa earned a Master’s degree in Strategic Communication from Texas Christian University (2022). Her graduate research focused on credit unions and corporate purpose. She also holds a B.S. from TCU in FTVDM and Advertising/Public Relations. Her career includes recognized work honored by programs such as the American Advertising Awards, CUNA Diamond Awards, CUES Golden Mirror Awards, and Cornerstone Credit Union League Pinnacle Awards.
