About Elisa

Transforming brands through strategic marketing excellence

As the owner and CEO of Kearley & Company, I lead a full-service marketing and branding firm that partners with financial institutions and healthcare clients across the United States.

My journey began in 2003 as a copywriter and strategist at Kearley & Company. Through dedication and strategic vision, I became Vice President in 2008 and purchased the firm in 2010. Today, I continue to lead as CEO while actively writing, consulting, speaking, and supporting our digital marketing initiatives.

I hold a Master's degree in Strategic Communication from Texas Christian University (2022), where my graduate research focused on credit unions and corporate purpose. I also earned a B.S. from TCU in FTVDM and Advertising/Public Relations.
